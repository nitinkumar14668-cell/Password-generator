What is a Strong Password Generator?

A Strong Password Generator is a free tool that helps you create secure passwords that are difficult to guess. A strong password usually includes uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. This makes it harder for hackers or automated bots to crack your password using brute force attacks.

Why Strong Passwords Matter

Most accounts get hacked because people use weak passwords like 123456, password, or their name and birth year. Strong passwords protect your social media accounts, email, gaming IDs, and even banking apps. Using a secure password is one of the best ways to stay safe online.
